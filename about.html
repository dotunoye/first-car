About APEX AUTO

Two decades of passion, expertise, and dedication to luxury automotive excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, APEX AUTO began with a simple vision: to connect luxury car enthusiasts with the world's finest automobiles. What started as a single showroom in Manhattan has grown into a premier destination for discerning buyers seeking the ultimate in automotive excellence.

Our founder, Marcus Sterling, spent decades in the luxury automotive industry, building relationships with manufacturers and collectors worldwide. This network became the foundation for APEX AUTO's unparalleled inventory and exclusive access to rare and limited-edition vehicles.

Today, APEX AUTO operates state-of-the-art facilities, employs certified specialists, and maintains a reputation for integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction that is unmatched in the industry.

Car Care Tips

Expert advice to keep your luxury vehicle in pristine condition.

Regular Oil Changes

Maintain engine health with regular oil changes. For luxury vehicles, high-quality synthetic oils are recommended every 7,500-10,000 miles.

Battery Care

Ensure your vehicle's battery is regularly tested and maintained. A weak battery can compromise starting performance and electronics.

Tire Maintenance

Check tire pressure monthly and rotate tires every 6,000 miles. Proper alignment ensures longevity and optimal performance.

Detailing

Professional detailing protects your car's paint and interior. Regular waxing and cleaning maintain the luxury appearance.

Brake Inspection

Regular brake inspections are crucial for safety. Have your braking system checked annually for optimal performance.

Seasonal Prep

Prepare your vehicle for seasonal changes. Winter tires, fluid adjustments, and protective treatments are recommended.
